    🤝 Donate Food & Support in Ireland

    These organisations help reduce food waste and support people in need across Ireland.

    🍎 FoodCloud

    Connects surplus food from businesses to charities and community groups.

    https://food.cloud/

    ❤️ Society of St. Vincent de Paul (SVP)

    Provides food support and helps families in need across Ireland.

    https://www.svp.ie/

    🍽️ Crosscare

    Offers food services, meals, and support for people experiencing poverty.

    https://crosscare.ie/

    🏠 Simon Community

    Supports people experiencing homelessness with food and housing services.

    https://www.simon.ie/

    🥣 Capuchin Day Centre

    Provides meals and food parcels for people in need in Dublin.

    https://capuchindaycentre.ie/

    🍲 Little Flower Penny Dinners

    Provides hot meals daily and support for elderly and vulnerable people.

    https://www.littleflower.ie/

    ⚠️ Always check each organisation’s website before donating food to ensure safety and requirements.
